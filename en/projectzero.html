Project Zero

Database for Multiple Schools

A central hub designed to combat academic laziness and streamline homework tracking.

The Problem

As the Vice Chairman of my class, I noticed a worrying trend: the rate of students not completing their homework was constantly rising. I realized the issue wasn't just laziness—it was a lack of organized access to assignments.

The Solution

I built this database to eliminate excuses. By providing a centralized, easy-to-access platform, I made it impossible for "I forgot" or "I didn't know" to be valid reasons for missing work. It’s my first step into web development, moving from Roblox Luau to real-world web logic.
